WHEN EVERYTHING FEELS URGENT
The 2-Minute Mental Reset

When your brain feels loud and you don't know what to do first:

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. GET EVERYTHING OUT

Say or write everything that's on your mind.
Don't organize it. Don't prioritize it.
Just get it all out.

Messy is perfect.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

2. WHAT ACTUALLY MATTERS TODAY?

Out of everything you just said, what actually matters today?

Not this week. Not eventually. Today.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

3. WHAT CAN WAIT?

What feels urgent—but isn't actually important right now?

Name it. Then set it aside.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

4. ONE SMALL NEXT STEP

What is the smallest next step that moves the thing that matters forward?

Not the whole plan. Just one step.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

5. THAT'S ENOUGH

You don't need to figure everything out today.
You just needed a place to start.

The rest can wait.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Come back to this anytime your brain feels overloaded.
